  • FRIEDNS SERIES

    September 22, 1994 - May 6, 2004

    Hello,
    This blog is about a classic series named Friends. It is about six friends (Monica, Ross, Chandler, Rachel, Phoebe and Joey) living in New York as they navigate their way through life and learn to grow up as they approach the third decade of their life. All, with the help from each other to get them through the obstacles that life naturally has for us. This series teaches us all about meaning of friendship, trust, caring, and also ways to overcome one's weakness and learinig from our terrible mistakes.
